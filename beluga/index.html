Beluga Whales

Belugas are a very unique species of whale. They are white and have long beaks. Native to Arctic regions, belugas feed on fish and squid in the icy waters. They are very intelligent and communicate through clicks, whistles, and chirps. Belugas have strong family bonds and hunt cooperatively. Baby belugas stay close to their mothers until they are two years old.
